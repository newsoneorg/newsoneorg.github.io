Amid ongoing turf war Kangana Ranaut gets best actress award

Known for her strong and non-stereotyped roles in several films and currently at loggerheads with those whom she calls Bollywood mafia Kangana Ranaut won the best actress award as the 67th National Film Awards for the year were announced today. As per an official release from Press Information Bureau (PIB), the actress got the award for her roles in Manikarnika-The Queen of Jhansi and Panga. The actress is presently enthusiastic about her role in Thalaivi, a biopic on Jayalalitha and MGR. The best actor award was shared by Dhanush and Manoj Bajpayee.
