Bhopal, May 28, 2021

Chief minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan on Friday cautioned administrative authorities and others saying even slight negligence was not warranted at this juncture when Corona infection was decline, as per an official statement from the state government.

Reviewing Covid situation in Sehore district Chouhan said, �Corona infection is under total control in the state. This has been made possible through the coordinated efforts of public representatives, government servants and common citizens. The positivity rate in the state is constantly on the decline. Even slight negligence can adversely affect all efforts, so caution and vigilance are necessary.�

He said, �The state government will provide every possible help to each victim�s family.�

�Unlock will begin from June 1 in a phased manner. For this, members of the crisis management committees at district, Janpad, village and ward levels will decide what to open and to what extent the exemption should be granted�, said the CM.

However, he added, Corona curfew should be strictly followed in the entire district. Crowded events like wedding ceremonies etc. should be avoided.

CM said maximum tests should be conducted. Mobile testing should also be conducted by making sector-wise action plan.

He said after identifying the persons coming in contact with Corona positive patients through contact tracing, they should be tested for Corona. This can prevent infection. Micro containment zones should be created in rural and urban areas with high infection. In such areas, the administration should identify the patients of cold, cough and fever and provide medical kits immediately and treat them. Crisis management committees should play an active role in this work. Home isolation arrangements for infected persons should be perfect. In case there is not enough space at home, these patients can be admitted to the Covid Care Centre.

He said all necessary preparations should be made to face the third wave of Corona infection. Corona protocol should be followed so strictly that the third wave does not come. Oxygen beds and ICU beds should be increased. Adequate oxygen supply must be ensured. �The state government will give subsidy in grant and electricity charges on setting up oxygen plant�, he added.

He said proper arrangements were also being made by the government to treat black fungus. Necessary medicines were also being arranged for the treatment.

�Kill-Corona campaign should be continued. Every person affected by cold, cough etc should be identified and provided with a medical kit immediately. Treatment should be arranged as needed. There should be no delay in identifying the symptoms of Corona and starting treatment. Corona patients admitted to hospitals should be treated better. Every effort should be made to save the patient�s life. Not a single dose of vaccine should go waste. According to scientists, vaccination is the most effective way to prevent Corona infection. Crisis management groups and social workers should clear the misconceptions against vaccination and bring awareness among the people.�

He said the first priority of the state government at this time was to save the lives of the people. �Corona breaks the body besides destroying you financially. Free Covid treatment should be ensured to all poor and middle class families under the Chief Minister Covid Upchar Yojana�, he added.

�The state government is making every effort to help poor families under the circumstances arising out of Corona. Two months ration has been provided by Prime Minister Narendra Modi and three months by the state government. Free ration is being distributed among all eligible beneficiaries. It should be ensured that no one goes hungry in the city. Necessary precautions should be taken to avoid Corona even during plucking of tendupatta. Funds are being released to the accounts of construction labourers and self-help groups. A decision has been taken to provide assistance of Rs one lakh the family which has lost its member due to Covid�, said the CM.
