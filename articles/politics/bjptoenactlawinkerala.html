BJP to enact Love Jihad law if voted to power in Kerala: Chouhan

Battle in Kerala

NEWSONE.ORG CORRESPONDENT
Bhopal, April 3, 2021

Madhya Pradesh chief minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan said in poll bound Kerala on Friday that BJP would enact a Love Jihad law over there if voted to power, as per BJP.

Madhya Pradesh government led by Chouhan has enacted a similar law here with a provision for harsh punishment in case of any conversion by way of lure, coercion or intimidation and also in case of inter-faith marriages by concealing one's identity.

Kerala will witness polling on April 6 for its state assembly. Counting of votes will take place on May 2 along with the results of West Bengal, Assam, Tamil Nadu and Pondicherry legislative assembly elections.

Addressing three public meetings at three different places Chouhan lashed out at the ruling LDF (Left Democratic Front), Congress led UDF (United Democratic Front) and also Congress MP Rahul Gandhi who represents Wayanad parliamentary constituency in Lok Sabha.

Chouhan who often uses acronyms of political opponents these days to attack them said, LDF means lawlessness, destructive and failed and UDF means unconstitutional, danger and fake.

Chouhan reiterated, R stands for rejected i.e. one who was rejected by the country, A stands for absent-minded, H is for hopeless, U stands for useless and L means liar i.e. one who often tells a lie.

Chouhan whose government recently entered into an agreement with Kerala to replicate its 'Responsible tourism' model here again used acronym in trying to convince the voters of Kerala that if BJP was voted to power it would usher Kerala to a new path of development and give a new identity to the South India state.

K - Knowledgeable Kerala, E - Energetic Kerala, R - Responsible Kerala, A - Able Kerala, L - Large-hearted Kerala and A - Aatmnirbhar Kerala, said the MP CM about the plan of the BJP to give a new identity to the state once it was voted to power.

He alleged, "The LDF and the UDF have converted Kerala which is known as the God's own country, into a land of scams and violence. Alternatively, they rule 5 years as these are two sides of the same coin which have no concern about development of the state.""

Chouhan said, "The LDF and the UDF have handed over Kerala into the hands of Jihadis. Here, Love-Jihad is going on and crimes are being committed to spoil many lives by misleading them through lure, enticement, intimidation and impersonation. I have come from the land of Madhya Pradesh. We have put in place a law against Love-Jihad. We are not against love but Jihad. Wherever BJP is there it will not allow Love-Jihad. The offenders of such a crime will be punished with 10 years imprisonment."

 

WHAT IS LOVE-JIHAD

As per Oxford dictionary Love-Jihad is "A holy war undertaken by Muslims against unbelievers. The name comes from Arabic jihād, literally "effort", expressing, in Muslim thought, struggle on behalf of God and Islam."
